Tyler Baudelaire

Tyler Baudilaire is the imaginative figure portrayed in Tyler, The Creator's latest album, Call Me if You Get Lost. This character, one of many creative alter-egos of LA-based rapper, producer, and creative, Tyler Okonma, is loosly based around the renowned French poet Charles Baudelaire. Tyler's brash and boisterous lyricism mirrors Baudelaire's poetry, often seeking to evoke emotions through vivid imaginitive language.
